   MY OPEN SOURCE MANIFESTO
   By Hima Agarwal | 24BCE10633
   Date: 28 March 2026
======================================

Every day, I rely on git — a tool built freely and shared openly by
developers who believed that knowledge belongs to everyone. To me,
freedom means independence. That is the spirit that drives open source:
the freedom to use, study, modify, and share without restriction.

If I could build anything and share it freely, I would build app.
Because standing on the shoulders of giants means giving back to
the community that made your work possible in the first place.

Open source is not just a license. It is a philosophy.
